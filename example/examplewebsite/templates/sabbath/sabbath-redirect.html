Sabbath Redirect

Hello and thank you for your interest in this website.

We value family and love our Heavenly Father, Yahweh and we choose to keep the Holy days and Feast days that He has given us to celebrate in the Bible. One of these Holy days, the weekly Sabbath, is a set-apart day of rest for Yahweh's people.

It is a blessing from Yahweh and a delight to have a day of rest so that we can grow closer to the One who made us and not forget what really matters. For these reasons, we do not have the majority of our website open to the public on these set-apart days.

You can come back on the first day of the week (commonly called "sunday") when our website will be open to the public again. Thank you for your understanding!

These Sabbath and Holy-day redirects are powered by the free & open-source software, Keep-Sabbath.
